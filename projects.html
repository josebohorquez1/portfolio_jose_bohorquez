Driver–Cyclist Awareness System

A senior design project focused on improving road safety by helping drivers detect nearby cyclists through wireless communication between vehicle-mounted and bicycle-mounted receivers.

  • Problem: Drivers often lack reliable awareness of cyclists, especially in low-visibility conditions.
  • Solution: Worked on a team to design two communicating receivers—a car unit and a bicycle unit—that transmit proximity and location-related signals.
  • Technologies: C, Arduino Microcontrollers
  • Highlights: Team collaboration, system integration, and safety-oriented design.

Budget Buddy

Accessible PDF Converter

A prototype tool designed to convert PDF documents into more accessible formats for a human computer interaction class project, improving usability for blind and screen reader users.

  • Problem: Many PDFs are inaccessible due to poor structure and tagging.
  • Solution: Created a converter that extracts and restructures content to HTML with accessibility in mind.
  • Technologies: Next JS, React, Node.js
  • Highlights: Focus on semantic structure, text extraction, and ease of use.

Statewide Chapter Website

  • Problem:Bringing in members to the chapter can be difficult especially if information is not centralized.
  • Solution: Build a website with chapter information that is accessible and allows prospective members to learn more about the chapter.
  • Technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript.
  • Highlights: Semantic HTML, keyboard navigation, and real stakeholder feedback.
